What is Xanedrock?

Xanedrock is a Minecraft Bedrock world created by DisphireMakes. It lets players experience life as a Lyoko Warrior—attending Kadic Academy while protecting the world from XANA’s attacks.

How does it work?

When XANA activates a tower, it begins launching hidden attacks on Earth. To stop them, players must enter Lyoko and fight XANA’s monsters to deactivate towers.

The game runs in waves. As the waves progress, XANA becomes stronger and the battles become harder. If a tower stays active for too long, XANA escapes the supercomputer—resulting in game over and a full restart.
