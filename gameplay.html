Gameplay

Each session begins when X.A.N.A. activates a tower somewhere in one of the five sectors of Lyoko, triggering hidden attacks on Earth that players must quickly understand and respond to.

To stop it, they enter Lyoko through the scanners, navigating its sectors while facing increasingly dangerous monsters guarding the active tower.

The longer the tower stays active, the closer X.A.N.A. gets to escaping the supercomputer. If it does, the world resets - creating a completely new scenario for the next attempt.

Monsters Recreated

To defend the towers, X.A.N.A. deploys monsters from the original series, including the Krab, Blok, Kankrelat, and Manta.

Each one behaves differently in combat, forcing players to adapt their strategy depending on which enemies stand between them and the tower.

Lyoko Warrior Abilities

Before entering Lyoko, players choose a Lyoko Warrior kit inspired by the abilities from the show.

Each kit changes how you fight and move inside the sectors, encouraging different playstyles and teamwork every time a tower is activated.
